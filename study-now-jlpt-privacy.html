Study Now:JLPT

Privacy Policy

A simple explanation of what information the app uses, where it is stored, and what choices users have.

Effective date: April 5, 2026
Developer: Kota Ogino
Contact: kotaba.app@gmail.com

1. Overview

Study Now:JLPT is an educational app that uses Apple’s Screen Time features to help users stay focused on JLPT study. The app lets users study JLPT content across levels N1 to N5, practice by category, manually add JLPT history, and track study progress.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, where that information is stored, and what choices users have.

2. Information the app uses

The app does not require account creation and does not ask users to log in.

The app may store the following information on the user’s device:

  • quiz history
  • JLPT level and study settings
  • study progress information
  • selected apps and restrictions configured through Apple’s Screen Time and FamilyControls features
  • purchase status needed to unlock premium features
  • JLPT history manually entered by the user inside the app

3. How information is stored

Based on the current version of the app, user data is stored only on the user’s device. The developer does not operate a backend service for storing user study data.

Because information is stored locally on the device, uninstalling the app, deleting app data, restoring a device, or changing devices may affect what data remains available unless Apple provides separate system-level restoration.

4. Permissions used by the app

Screen Time / FamilyControls

The app requests access to Apple’s Screen Time and FamilyControls features so the user can choose which apps to restrict while studying. The selected apps are chosen by the user.

Notifications

The app may request notification permission to remind users about study sessions or app-related events. Notification permission is optional and can be managed in device settings.

5. Third-party services and Apple frameworks

The app uses Apple technologies and system frameworks, including:

  • StoreKit / Apple In-App Purchase for subscriptions and restoring purchases
  • Screen Time / FamilyControls to support app restriction features selected by the user
  • the default email app when the user chooses to send feedback

Payment processing for subscriptions is handled by Apple. The developer does not receive or store full payment card information.

6. Advertising, tracking, and analytics

Study Now:JLPT does not include ads.

The developer does not sell personal information and does not share personal information for cross-app or cross-site advertising.

Based on the current version, the app does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not use user data for personalized advertising.

7. Feedback and support messages

If a user taps a feedback or support button, the app may open the user’s default email app. Any information the user includes in that email is provided voluntarily by the user.

8. User controls

Users can use the app without creating an account.

Users may be able to delete JLPT history they add to the app. Users may also manage permissions such as notifications and Screen Time access through iOS settings or Apple’s permission flows.

According to the current app design, users cannot fully reset JLPT progress inside the app.

9. Children’s privacy

The app is intended for a general audience. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children through a user account system or backend profile database. If you believe a child has provided personal information by email or through another direct contact channel, please contact kotaba.app@gmail.com.

10. International users

The app may be used globally. Because data is stored locally on the user’s device and not in a separate developer-operated backend for app data, many privacy questions will depend on the user’s own device environment and Apple account settings.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

The developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the app, legal requirements, or business operations. The updated version will be posted on the app’s website with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or privacy-related requests, contact kotaba.app@gmail.com.
