Study Now:JLPT

Focus-first JLPT study with Screen Time tools

Study Now:JLPT helps users stay focused by combining structured JLPT study tools with iOS Screen Time restrictions chosen by the user.

Developer: Kota Ogino
Support: kotaba.app@gmail.com
Status: Coming soon

What the app does

The app uses Apple’s Screen Time and FamilyControls frameworks so users can restrict selected apps until they study using Study Now:JLPT.

Study coverage

Practice JLPT content across N1 to N5, with category-based study for kanji, vocabulary, and grammar.

Progress tools

Track study progress, manually add JLPT history inside the app, and review your study settings locally on device.

Features

  • JLPT study support for all levels from N1 to N5
  • Category practice for kanji, vocabulary, and grammar
  • Focus support using iOS Screen Time restrictions selected by the user
  • Study progress tracking and manually entered JLPT history
  • Free and premium feature tiers
